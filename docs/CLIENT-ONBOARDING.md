# Client Onboarding Questionnaire

Everything we need to configure and deploy your AI Sales Agent.

**Fastest way:** Record a 15-minute voice note answering these questions. We'll extract everything we need.

---

## 1. Company Basics
- Company name
- Tagline / one-liner (what you do in one sentence)
- Website URL
- Target market (who are your ideal clients?)

## 2. Services & Pricing
- List each service you offer
- Price range for each (e.g., "Starting at $500" or "$200-500/month")
- Who is each service ideal for?

## 3. What Makes You Different
- Top 3-5 things that set you apart from competitors
- Any stats or proof points? (e.g., "Average delivery in 5 days", "98% satisfaction rate")

## 4. Common Objections
How should the bot handle these? (Give us your best response for each)
- "Too expensive" / "Out of my budget"
- "I need to think about it"
- "I do it myself / in-house"
- "I had a bad experience with [similar service]"
- "Not the right time"

## 5. Process
Walk us through your process step by step:
- First contact → what happens?
- How does the project/service work?
- What's the typical timeline?
- What does the client need to provide?

## 6. FAQ (Top 10-20)
The most common questions people ask you. Include your answers.

## 7. Social Proof
- Testimonials (names + quotes)
- Notable clients or projects
- Awards, certifications, press mentions

## 8. Portfolio / Past Work
- Examples of past work (links, descriptions, or case studies)
- Before/after results if applicable

## 9. What You DON'T Do
Important boundaries for the bot:
- Services you don't offer
- Types of clients you don't serve
- Questions the bot should NOT try to answer

## 10. Tone & Personality
- How should the bot sound? (casual/professional/friendly/authoritative)
- Any specific vocabulary for your industry?
- Language preference (English, Portuguese, Spanish, etc.)

## 11. Brand Assets
- Primary brand color (hex code, e.g., #2563eb)
- Secondary/accent color
- Logo URL (optional — for future use)

## 12. Calendar & Booking
- Available times for discovery calls
- Call duration preference (15/30/45/60 min)
- Cal.com, Calendly, or other booking tool?
- Booking link (if already set up)

## 13. Notifications
- Where should you receive new lead alerts? (Telegram / Email / Both)
- Email address for notifications
- Telegram username (if applicable)

## 14. Website Access
- How to add code to your website? (WordPress admin / Wix / Webflow / other)
- Do you need help adding the widget code?

---

## After You Send This

1. We configure the AI agent with your info
2. We run 10-15 test conversations to tune the responses
3. We embed the widget on your site
4. You get a Google Sheet with all captured leads
5. You start receiving notifications for every qualified lead

**Timeline:** 2-3 business days from receiving your materials to live widget.
