"An AI sales agent that lives on your website, qualifies leads, pitches your services, and books calls on your calendar — 24/7, fully automated."

1. **Service businesses** (agencies, consultancies, coaching) — high ticket, need qualified calls
2. **B2B SaaS** — need demo bookings
3. **Professional services** (law firms, accounting, real estate) — need consultations
4. **Recruitment firms** — need client acquisition (eat your own dog food with AnimaRH)

## Sales Pitch (30 seconds)

"Right now, visitors hit your website and leave without talking to anyone. What if you had an AI sales rep that greets every visitor, understands their needs, pitches your services, and books qualified calls on your calendar — 24/7? No more lost leads. Our clients see 3-5x more booked calls within the first month. Want to see a demo?"

## Demo Flow

1. Show the chat widget on a demo site
2. Walk through a real conversation (discovery → pitch → booking)
3. Show the Google Sheet filling up in real-time
4. Show the Telegram notification popping up
5. Show Cal.com booking confirmation
6. Ask: "How many calls per month would make this worth $497?"

## Objection Handling (for YOUR sales conversations)

- "I already have a chatbot" → "Most chatbots are FAQ machines. This one actually sells — it discovers needs, pitches services, handles objections, and books calls. It's an AI salesperson, not a chatbot."
- "AI isn't personal enough" → "We train it on YOUR services, YOUR tone, YOUR objection handling. Prospects can't tell it's AI. And it never sleeps, never has a bad day."
- "Too expensive" → "One extra booked call per month pays for the entire service. How much is a new client worth to you?"
- "What if it says something wrong?" → "It only knows what we program. It can't make promises or quote prices beyond what you approve. And you can review all conversations."
